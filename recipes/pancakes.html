Pancakes Recipe

What are Pancakes?

A pancake is a flat cake, often thin and round, prepared from a starch-based batter that may contain eggs, milk and butter and cooked on a hot surface such as a griddle or frying pan, often frying with oil or butter.

Ingredients

Serves 4

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 3/4 cup milk
  • 1 tsp vanilla essence
  • 2 cups self-raining flour
  • 1/3 cup caster sugar
  • Butter, for frying, plus extra to serve
  • Maple syrup, to serve

Steps

  1. Whisk eggs , milk and vanilla together in a jug. Sift flour into a large bowl. Stir in sugar . Make a well in the centre. Add milk mixture. Whisk until just combined.
  2. Heat a large non-stick frying pan over medium heat. Grease pan with butter or spray with cooking oil. Using 1/4 cup mixture per pancake, cook 2 pancakes for 2 minutes or until bubbles appear on surface. Turn and cook for a further 1-2 minutes or until cooked through.
  3. Transfer to a plate. Cover loosely with foil to keep warm. Repeat with remaining mixture, greasing pan with butter or cooking oil between batches.
  4. Serve with maple syrup and extra butter.
