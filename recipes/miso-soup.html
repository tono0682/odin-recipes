Miso Soup Recipe

What is Miso Soup?

Miso soup is a staple in Japanese cuisine. We enjoy it almost every single day, whether it be for breakfast, lunch, or dinner!

Ingredients

Serves 4

  • 1 sachet of dashi
  • 600mL water
  • 200g silken tofu
  • 4 Tbsp miso (red/white)
  • 1 Tbsp dried wakame seaweed
  • 1 spring onion

Steps

  1. Cut spring onion into thin rounds
  2. Add water and dashi to saucepan. Bring it to simmer over medium heat and turn off the heat
  3. Add the miso. Put the miso onto a tablespoon, lower into water and stir with chopsticks to dissolve completely.
  4. Cut tofu into cubes and add to miso soup
  5. Add spring onions
  6. Serve immeidately
